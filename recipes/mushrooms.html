Magical Panko Mushrooms

Description

These divinely inspired, garlicky, parmesan-ey, panko crusted & oven-baked mushrooms don't require a fryer!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 30g parmesan grated
  • 1 t garlic powder
  • 1/2 t salt
  • 1 t black pepper
  • 1 T dried parsley
  • 1 - 2 eggs depending on size
  • 20 mushrooms

Method

  1. Preheat to 230°C & line baking sheet with parchment paper; set aside.
  2. In a bowl combine breadcrumbs, salt, pepper, garlic powder, parmesan, & parsley. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg whites.
  3. Dip each mushroom in egg whites, then roll mushroom through the breadcrumb mixture, & place on baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10 – 15 minutes or until golden brown & crispy then serve with ranch dressing, marinara or dipping sauce of your choice!
